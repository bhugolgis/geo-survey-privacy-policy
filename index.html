Geo-Survey Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18-09-2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Geo-Survey. We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app for geotagging trees and structures.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

2.1 Location Data

To provide accurate geotagging of trees and structures, Geo-Survey requires access to your precise location data. This is essential for tagging the correct geographical coordinates of the objects you survey.

2.2 Data Retention

We do not store or retain any personal user data apart from the geotagged information related to trees and structures. Once the tagging process is complete, we do not keep any location data or other personal information.

2.3 Data Use

Your location data is used in real-time solely for the purpose of geotagging trees and structures within the app. We do not use your location data for any other purposes.

3. Data Sharing

3.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your location data or any geotagged information with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively within the app to provide the services you request.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your geotagged data during its use. However, since we do not retain personal user data, there is no stored data that requires long-term security protection
